The Franchisee Model
From 2001, a franchise model has been adopted. Since then there are multiple outlets opened across Chennai, Bangalore, Vizag, Pondicherry and Coimbatore.The Red Box sets up the outlet and then hands it over to the franchisee along with the staff. The initial investment to open an outlet is between ₹ 22 lakhs - ₹ 25 lakhs with a Return on investment at around 1.8 years. Outlets are typically 600 to 800 sq. ft, and they are takeaway outlets with kitchens.
Bejesh Restaurant Menu The menu includes a wide assortment of starters, mains, desserts and drinks including combo options that serve rice/drinks with a gravy. This menu is priced between ₹35 to ₹250. The recipes of these are specifically curated to manage large orders in a short span of time.
